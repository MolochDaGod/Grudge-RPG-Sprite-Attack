Combat System

Stamina Resource

3-pip bar. Dash costs 3, ranged costs 1, specials cost 2. Regens 1 per 3 seconds. Exhaustion punishes spam — manage your resources or die.

Super Attacks

Build meter through combat. When full, trigger a cinematic freeze-frame super with radial burst and massive damage. Each of the 39 Fighters has a unique super.

Dash & Parry

LMB dash lunges based on stamina. RMB parry reflects damage back. Perfect timing wins fights — not button mashing.

AI Opponent

Intelligent AI that approaches, combos, dodges projectiles, blocks, and uses supers. It will read you.

Online PvP

Room-based matchmaking. Create a room, share the code, fight anyone. Real-time input relay over WebSocket.

39 Fighters

From Knight to Necromancer. Barbarian to Wanderer Magician. Each with unique sprites, stats, attack animations, and projectiles.
